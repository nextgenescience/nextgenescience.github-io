Projects & Outcomes



Currently, there are 1,004 enterprises identified for low-carbon and warm-value initiatives. Among these, 100 have been authorized as child-friendly assessment agencies, and another 100 have been recognized for their contributions to warm-value labeling and environmentally friendly practices.

10 NATIONAL GROUPS & 100+ TYPES PRODUCTS RECOGNITION
Since 2018, CEESTA has successively released 10 groups, including the General Rules for Evaluating the Carbon Footprint of Electrical and Electronic Products, Carbon Labels for LED Road Lighting Products, Carbon Footprint Evaluation of Electrical and Electronic Products Part
2: Televisions, and General Rules for Evaluating the Carbon Labels of Enterprises.At present, we are organizing and participating in the formulation of 69 nationa groups, and plan to complete the formulation of carbon footprint evaluation for 100+ types of products or services by 2025.

Through collaboration with over 100 schools, we have developed our own low-carbon textbooks, laboratories, educational bases, and schools. Each year, we also host winter and summer camps focused on carbon awareness, as well as a carbon-neutral youth innovation and entrepreneurship competition.
