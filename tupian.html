About Us

NextGene is a testament to the transformative power of education and innovation in the fight against climate change. Our journey commenced in the heart of London, spurred by the collective vision of young scientists and visionaries from prestigious institutions worldwide, including Cambridge, Oxford, Stanford, Harvard, and MIT. Inspired by the urgent environmental crises unfolding globally—from the Amazon fires and European heatwaves to the floods ravaging India—we recognized the growing concern and desire for actionable knowledge among the youth.

Our foundation was built on a crucial realization: while 95% of young people are worried about climate change, a significant portion questions the adequacy of their education on the matter. This gap between concern and knowledge sparked our mission to democratize access to scientifically rigorous, peer-reviewed climate education, distilled into engaging, bite-sized content. As we embarked on this mission, our efforts quickly resonated with a global audience, attracting a dedicated team passionate about making climate education accessible and understandable for all.

Today, NextGene Innovations stands as a beacon of hope and action, growing beyond its initial focus to encompass a broad spectrum of sustainability and green living education. Through a series of strategic partnerships, innovative educational programs, and community engagement, we've laid down significant milestones. From establishing comprehensive carbon science education systems in schools worldwide to launching free educational resources on sustainable development, our achievements are numerous. Yet, at the core of our efforts is the belief that an informed and engaged youth is the key to unlocking a more sustainable future.

As we continue to evolve, our commitment remains steadfast: to improve climate understanding and education globally, fostering a world that rewards curiosity, critical thinking, exploration, and learning. A world where everyone is empowered to explore their passions and contribute towards a sustainable future. Our story is not just about climate education; it's about creating a world with less suffering and more opportunity, where the full potential of human problem-solving is unleashed in the service of the planet.

Join us at NextGene Innovations as we forge ahead, crafting a greener, more sustainable world for generations to come. Together, we can turn the tide against climate change and pave the way for a brighter, more resilient future. The journey begins here. Join the movement!

The NextGene team unites over a commitment to low-carbon education. In China, by collaborating with the Ministry of Education and the Beijing Municipal Science & Technology Commission, we've established an extensive carbon literacy program in schools. This includes integrating low-carbon textbooks, audiobooks, and labs, training qualified teachers, and launching a youth carbon neutrality innovation competition to boost student engagement. We've formed partnerships with over 100 schools in the UK and China.

To broaden our impact, we partnered with the UAE Ministry of Education to create the Global Green Partnership, attracting countries like the USA, UK, France, and Germany. Following the successful release of a white paper after COP28, we're preparing to publish a white paper on the outcomes of low-carbon popular science education.

In April 2024, we're honored to attend the ECOSOC Youth Forum at the United Nations headquarters in New York. There, we'll host a forum to amplify the voices of youth and showcase their achievements in the low-carbon sector. Our journey highlights the power of youth-led initiatives in driving the agenda for a sustainable future.
